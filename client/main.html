Purpose of the group

The purpose of the Advanced Options and Hedging Strategies group is to learn more about smart investment trading with a focus on using stock options. Options can provide a huge amount of flexibility to the trader to generate profit or hedge against losses. This group is formed of a whole bunch of friendly people willing to share ideas, and offer constructive criticism to help you generate gains and protect against losses.

Options Definitions and Strategies

What is an option, and how do they work? Here's a nice overview.

Options are flexible. You can go long, go short. You can buy or sell options. You can choose your own expiration and strike price. Because of this flexibility, you can trade options in different combinations to take advantage of market trends.
